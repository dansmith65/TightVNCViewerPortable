TightVNC Donations
 

If you use and like TightVNC, please consider making a donation.

We accept payments via the Kagi Internet store. You can donate any amount of your choice starting at $10, and there are many payment methods available: credit cards, PayPal, checks, wire transfers etc.

Go to the payment page at Kagi.(opens in a new window)

  • ... have questions regarding donations;

  • ... are interested in supporting our development efforts in some other way.
Contributors get more support!
If you have made a donation recently, your e-mail messages will be read and answered before processing the rest of e-mail. Even if we are overloaded with mail, we can see that there are unread messages from contributors, whose e-mail addresses correspond to those specified in the payment details.

Thank you in advance!
