vncviewer and Xvnc now can use either system zlib and JPEG libraries,
or ones packaged within TightVNC source archive in the lib/ directory.

these libraries for those who don't have them installed in the system,
and another version without libraries, directly from CVS, for those
who do have zlib and/or JPEG libraries installed. In the former case,
build procedure would include additional "make libs" step. System
libraries will be linked dynamically, libraries included in the source
archive will be linked in statically.
